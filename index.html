Why Adopting a Dog Changes Everything

Adopting a shelter dog brings companionship, saves lives, and lets you welcome a unique personality into your home, often for less than the cost of buying. Pets from shelters are typically vaccinated, spayed/neutered, and matched to your family so you get a loving, healthy companion.
